**Ma Bell ESP32 Mobile Bluetooth Gateway**

A DIY hardware and firmware project that transforms a classic rotary telephone into a fully functional Bluetooth handset.

Features:
- **Rotary dialing** and **real analog ringer**
- **ESP32-based Bluetooth HFP interface**
- Audio routed over **PCM/I2S**
- Seamless pairing with mobile phones

Whether you're retrofitting your home with vintage charm or exploring telephony internals, this project bridges **1970s Bell System design** with **modern embedded development**.
